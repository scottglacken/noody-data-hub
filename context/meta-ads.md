# Meta Ads — Noody Skincare Context

## Business Context
- Noody Skincare: NZ-based children's skincare brand
- DTC is ~41% of revenue ($383K/yr), wholesale 45%, international 14%
- Meta is the primary paid acquisition channel (80%+ of ad budget)

## Creative Testing Framework
- Always have 3-5 active creative tests running
- Test structure: 1 hero creative + 2-3 variants (different hooks, formats)
- Best performers historically: UGC testimonials from NZ parents, before/after skin results, product-in-use lifestyle shots
- Video outperforms static 2:1 on average
- Winning hook patterns: problem-first ("Is your child's skin..."), social proof ("10,000+ NZ families trust...")

## Advantage+ Settings
- Use Advantage+ Shopping campaigns for broad prospecting
- Advantage+ Audience for retargeting
- Minimum 7-day attribution window for children's skincare (longer consideration cycle)
- Exclude existing customers from prospecting campaigns

## Audience Notes
- Core audience: NZ parents/caregivers of children 0-12
- Key interests: natural skincare, eczema, sensitive skin, parenting
- Lookalike audiences based on purchasers perform best
- Geographic focus: NZ primary, AU secondary

## Benchmarks (What Good Looks Like for Noody)
- ROAS target: >2.5x (last-click)
- CPP (Cost Per Purchase): <$25 ideal, <$35 acceptable
- DTC MER (Meta spend / DTC revenue): <35%
- CTR: >1.5% on prospecting, >2.5% on retargeting
- Frequency: <2.0 on prospecting, <4.0 on retargeting

## Historical Best Performers
- Black Friday/Cyber Monday: ROAS peaks at 5-8x with bundle offers
- Winter campaigns (Jun-Aug): Higher conversion due to eczema season
- July 2025 sale: $24K+ single day, ROAS >7x
- November 2025 BFCM: $45K+ single day peak

## Common Issues
- Platform concentration risk: 80%+ spend on Meta
- Summer months (Dec-Feb): Lower ROAS as skincare demand drops
- Creative fatigue: Refresh every 2-3 weeks
- Attribution: Meta over-reports by ~20-30% vs Shopify actual

## Strategic Notes
- DTC ads may create halo effect driving wholesale (Farmers) sales
- Consider: is the true value of Meta spend higher than DTC MER suggests?
- Diversification target: 70% Meta / 20% Google / 10% TikTok within 6 months
