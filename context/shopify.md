# Shopify (DTC) — Noody Skincare Context

## Revenue Per Visit — The Grand Equalizer
**RPV = Conversion Rate x AOV**

RPV is the single most important metric for DTC profitability. It tells you how much revenue each visitor to your store generates.

Why it's the "grand equalizer":
- You can't control Meta's CPM or CPC
- You CAN control conversion rate and AOV
- Higher RPV means every ad click is worth more
- Higher RPV directly reduces effective CPP and improves MER
- **RPV improvements compound**: 10% CR lift + 10% AOV lift = 21% RPV lift (1.1 x 1.1 = 1.21)

### Key Conversion Levers (Priority Order)
1. **Product page optimization**: Clear hero image, benefits-first copy, social proof, trust badges
2. **Site speed**: Every 1-second delay reduces conversion by 7%
3. **Mobile experience**: 70%+ of traffic is mobile — design mobile-first
4. **Social proof**: Reviews, UGC, testimonials, "X people bought this today"
5. **Trust signals**: Money-back guarantee, secure checkout badges, clear returns policy
6. **Simplified checkout**: Fewer steps, guest checkout, auto-fill, multiple payment options
7. **Cart recovery**: Exit-intent popups, abandoned cart emails
8. **Free shipping threshold**: Set ~$10 above current AOV

## AOV Optimization
Every $10 AOV increase drops DTC MER by ~2-3 percentage points. Strategies:
- **Bundles**: Product bundles at 10-20% discount vs individual (biggest lever)
- **Upsells**: Post-add-to-cart recommendations ("Customers also bought...")
- **Cross-sells**: Complementary products on PDP and in cart drawer
- **Free shipping threshold**: Set strategically to drive larger orders
- **Gift with purchase**: Add value without discounting (protects brand)
- **Tiered pricing**: Spend $X get Y% off (encourages larger carts)

## The 6-Week Game Plan
Structured approach to improving the equation:

### Week 1-2: Calibrate
- Fill Clarity Sheet with 3+ months of data
- Identify Situation (1-5) and Constraint (Bandwidth/Efficiency/Throughput)
- Set traffic light benchmarks for your business
- Audit ad account, email flows, and website

### Week 3-4: Quick Wins
- Fix lowest-hanging fruit from calibration
- Common: fix broken flows, pause villain ads, set up browse abandonment, optimize free shipping threshold
- Each quick win must be measurable against the equation

### Week 5-6: Scale Test
- If equation is profitable (Situation 1), run a Scale Challenge
- Increase ad spend 20-30% for 7-14 days
- Monitor equation daily
- Evaluate against calibration baselines
