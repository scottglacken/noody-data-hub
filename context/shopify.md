# Shopify (DTC) — Noody Skincare Context

## Business Context
- DTC channel: ~41% of total revenue ($383K/yr from Xero)
- Shopify is the sole DTC platform
- DTC carries all ad spend but only ~41% of revenue — true MER is higher than blended

## Product Lineup
### Core Products
- **Calm Balm** — Hero product, targets eczema/sensitive skin, highest margin
- **Lotion Potion** — Daily moisturizer, high repeat purchase rate
- **Soft Suds** — Gentle wash/shampoo, entry point product
- **Bedtime Bestie** — Nighttime skincare routine, good for bundles
- **Sun Balm** — Sun protection, seasonal (summer peak)

## Pricing Strategy
- Individual products: $25-$45 NZD range
- Bundles drive AOV uplift
- Free shipping threshold should be set ~$10 above current AOV to incentivize larger carts

## Bundle Strategy
- **Complete Care Bundle**: Calm Balm + Lotion Potion + Soft Suds (~$89)
- **Bedtime Bundle**: Lotion Potion + Bedtime Bestie (~$65)
- **New Baby Bundle**: Entry pack for new parents
- Bundles should target AOV of $75-$90
- Cross-sell on PDP and in cart drawer

## AOV Targets
- Current benchmark: >$70
- Target: $75-$85
- Levers: Bundles, free shipping threshold, cart upsells, post-purchase upsell
- Every $10 AOV increase drops DTC MER by ~2-3 percentage points

## Conversion Rate Benchmarks
- Overall store CR: >2.5% (good), >3.5% (excellent)
- Add-to-cart rate: >8%
- Cart-to-checkout: >45%
- Checkout completion: >60%

## Key Metrics (RPV = CR × AOV)
- Revenue Per Visit (RPV) is the key DTC lever
- Improving either CR or AOV improves RPV
- RPV improvements compound: 10% CR lift + 10% AOV lift = 21% RPV lift

## Shipping
- Shipping as % of revenue: track and minimize
- Free shipping threshold: should be strategic (drives AOV)
- NZ domestic shipping: negotiate courier rates at volume

## Seasonal Patterns
- Winter (Jun-Aug): Peak eczema season, highest organic demand
- November: BFCM peak ($45K+ days possible)
- December: Christmas gifting
- January-February: Slower period, focus on retention and email
- Summer: Sun Balm push, lower overall skincare demand

## Known Issues
- High proportion of single-item orders reduces AOV
- Need stronger bundle visibility on homepage and PDPs
- Cart abandonment rate needs monitoring
- Post-purchase flow may be inactive — critical for repeat purchases
